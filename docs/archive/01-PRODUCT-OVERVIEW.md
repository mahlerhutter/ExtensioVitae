# EXTENSIOVITAE — Product Overview

## What It Is

ExtensioVitae is a personalized longevity planning system that generates a science-backed 30-day blueprint in under 3 minutes. Users complete a focused intake, receive an AI-generated daily action plan across 6 longevity pillars, and get reinforced through calm, precise WhatsApp nudges each morning.

## Who It's For

High-performing professionals aged 35–65 who:
- Are time-poor but health-conscious
- Want clarity, not complexity
- Are biohacking-curious but skeptical of hype
- Value premium, minimal experiences
- Need accountability without noise

## Core Promise

**One sentence:** *Get a personalized, science-informed 30-day longevity blueprint delivered to your WhatsApp in under 3 minutes—then actually follow through.*

## Why It Wins (Differentiation)

| Competitors | ExtensioVitae |
|-------------|---------------|
| Generic wellness apps | Hyper-personalized to your context |
| Information overload | 6 pillars, ≤30 min/day |
| App fatigue | Comes to you via WhatsApp |
| Gamification gimmicks | Calm, professional, no badges |
| Medical claims | Lifestyle optimization only |
| Complex onboarding | 3-minute intake, instant plan |

## The 6 Longevity Pillars

1. **Sleep** — Recovery architecture
2. **Movement** — Strategic physical stress
3. **Nutrition** — Fuel timing and quality
4. **Stress** — Nervous system regulation
5. **Connection** — Social and purpose alignment
6. **Environment** — Toxin reduction and optimization

## Design Philosophy

- **Calm over chaotic** — Apple-like restraint
- **Clarity over features** — Do one thing well
- **Trust over hype** — No medical claims, transparent methodology
- **Execution over education** — Tell them what to do, not why (unless asked)

## Business Model (MVP)

- **Phase 1:** Free beta (email + phone capture)
- **Phase 2:** Paid tier with extended plans, deeper personalization
- **Phase 3:** B2B wellness programs

## Success Metrics (First 100 Users)

1. Intake completion rate >80%
2. WhatsApp opt-in rate >70%
3. Day 7 check-in response rate >50%
4. NPS >40
